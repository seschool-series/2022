Welcome!

The 2020 International Summer School on Software Engineering hosted at the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano, Italy on July 7-10, 2020 is an event  featuring tutorials delivered by leading researchers in the Software Engineering field.

The sixth edition of the school is organised by the Software and Systems Engineering Research Group at unibz and the XXX. The school is intended both for practitioners and researchers.

This-year theme is AI and Continuous Software Engineering.

Participants will also enjoy the beauty of the nearby Dolomites from the town and during the SeSchool excursion.
